Welcome to VASA Group

Building Mozambique Through Unified Excellence

VASA Group is a diversified multi-industry business group operating across Mozambique. With a unified ownership structure, we drive growth and innovation across six strategic sectors, ensuring professional standards and nationwide impact in every operation we undertake.

Our Mission

To provide integrated, high-quality industrial and commercial solutions that empower Mozambican communities and businesses through reliability, innovation, and unwavering professional standards.

Our Vision

To be the leading diversified business group in Mozambique, recognized for setting the benchmark in cross-industry excellence and sustainable economic contribution nationwide.

Core Values

  • Loyalty: Committed to our partners and people.
  • Affordability: Value-driven solutions for all.
  • Trustworthiness: Integrity in every transaction.
  • Quality: Excellence as our baseline standard.
  • Professionalism: Expert governance and execution.

Corporate Profile

VASA Group operates under a unified ownership model, providing a stable and cohesive governance structure that ensures strategic alignment across all six operating companies. Our group is built on a foundation of reliability and structured operations, allowing us to manage complex multi-industry requirements with precision and efficiency.

From our headquarters in Nacala, we oversee a nationwide network that integrates transport, agriculture, construction, automotive services, customs clearing, and specialized hygiene services. This integrated approach allows us to leverage synergies across the group, delivering superior value and consistent professional standards to every client we serve in Mozambique.
