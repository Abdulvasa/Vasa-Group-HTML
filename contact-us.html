Support & FAQs

Common questions about contacting VASA Group

What is the typical response time?

We operate with a 24-hour response promise. Most inquiries submitted through our central hub are addressed within one business day by the relevant department head.

Which company should I contact for logistics?

For heavy haulage and transport, contact VASA Trading Transport. For customs clearing and forwarding, MOZEX is your dedicated partner. You can select both in our inquiry form for integrated services.

Do you have offices outside of Nacala?

Our main administrative hub is in Nacala, but we maintain operational points and site offices across Mozambique to support nationwide projects and logistics routes.

Can I request a quote for multiple services?

Absolutely. Use the "General Inquiry" or "Partnership" option in the form and specify the range of services required in the description field. Our group coordinator will handle your request.
