Our structured operations are built on a foundation of rigorous project management, ensuring that every residential, commercial, and industrial development we undertake meets international safety and quality standards.

With over 8 years of experience in the Mozambican market, we have refined our commitment to quality and safety. Our team of skilled professionals utilizes modern equipment and proven methodologies to deliver projects on time and within budget, fostering trust and long-term partnerships with our clients nationwide.

Operations & Capacity

Strategic deployment and resource management across Mozambique.

Service Area

Headquartered in Nacala, we provide nationwide coverage across Mozambique, with active projects in Maputo, Beira, and Nampula.

Operating Hours

Standard site operations: 07:00 – 17:00 (Mon-Fri). Emergency structural support and critical site management available 24/7.

Fleet & Equipment

Proprietary inventory of heavy machinery including excavators, concrete mixers, and scaffolding systems for large-scale projects.

Project Timelines

Average residential turnaround: 6–12 months. Commercial shells: 4–8 months. We prioritize efficient phased delivery to minimize downtime.

Pricing & Contracts

Transparent quotation framework for every structural requirement.

Quotation-Based Pricing

All projects at Elite Builders are priced via a detailed quotation process. This ensures accuracy and fair market value based on current material costs and technical specifications. We provide a transparent breakdown of labor, materials, and overheads.

Factors Affecting Project Cost

Total investment is determined by several key factors including site location (logistics), soil conditions (foundation complexity), material finishes (standard vs. luxury), and required completion timeline (expedited vs. standard).

Contract Availability & Terms

We offer various contract structures including Lump Sum, Cost-Plus, and Design-Build. All contracts include milestone-based payment schedules, performance guarantees, and comprehensive insurance coverage.

Sample Scope of Works

Typical scope items include: Site clearance, foundation works, super-structure (brick/concrete), roofing, electrical & plumbing rough-ins, internal finishes, and external landscaping/paving.
