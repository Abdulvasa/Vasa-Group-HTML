MOZ FUMIGATION

Advanced Protection, Absolute Safety

MOZ FUMIGATION provides expert pest control and sanitation services across Mozambique. With a safety-first approach and cutting-edge technology, we protect your residential, commercial, and agricultural assets from infestations, ensuring a clean and secure environment nationwide.

Reliability in Every Spray

MOZ FUMIGATION is a premier provider of professional pest management and sanitation solutions, operating as a key entity within the VASA Group. We pride ourselves on our structured operations and unwavering reliability, serving as a trusted partner for businesses and homeowners across Mozambique. Our mission is to deliver high-standard fumigation services that prioritize both environmental safety and client health.

Our team consists of highly trained technicians who are fully licensed and compliant with national health and safety regulations. By utilizing industry-leading equipment and certified chemicals, we ensure that every engagement meets rigorous quality standards. As part of VASA Group's unified excellence, we bring professional standards and a 24-hour response promise to every project we undertake.
