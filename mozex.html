About MOZEX

MOZEX stands as a pillar of reliability in Mozambique's logistics landscape, specializing in complex customs clearing and structured trade operations. With years of deep-rooted expertise, we navigate the intricacies of import and export regulations to provide a seamless experience for businesses of all sizes. Our commitment to compliance ensures that every shipment meets statutory requirements, minimizing delays and maximizing operational efficiency.

As part of the VASA Group, we leverage a nationwide network and multi-industry synergy to offer benefits that go beyond simple brokerage. Importers and exporters gain a strategic partner dedicated to transparency, speed, and professional excellence. From the Port of Nacala to borders across the country, MOZEX is the engine that keeps your supply chain moving forward without friction.

Pricing & Quotations

Transparent cost structures tailored to your specific trade requirements.

Quotation-Based Pricing Model

Due to the variable nature of customs duties, taxes, and handling fees, MOZEX operates on a quotation-based model. This ensures that you receive a precise cost breakdown specific to your cargo type, volume, and destination, avoiding hidden charges.

Factors Affecting Clearance Costs

Final pricing is influenced by several factors: commodity classification (HS Codes), cargo value, port of entry, urgency level, and any additional specialized permits or inspections required by Mozambican authorities.

Turnaround Times & Delivery

We pride ourselves on industry-leading turnaround times. Standard clearances are typically processed within 24-48 hours of document submission, depending on port congestion and authority response times.

Contract & Service-Level Availability

For high-volume importers and exporters, we offer Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and long-term contracts. These provide priority handling, fixed management fees, and dedicated account management for consistent trade flow.
