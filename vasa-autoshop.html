Our Services

About the Autoshop

VASA Autoshop is a cornerstone of the VASA Group's commitment to industrial excellence in Mozambique. We operate a state-of-the-art facility designed to handle everything from individual passenger vehicles to complex commercial fleets. Our mission is to redefine automotive care through structured operations, qualified technicians, and a relentless focus on safety compliance.

Every vehicle that enters our workshop is treated with precision and transparency. We utilize advanced diagnostic tools and adhere to international service standards, providing our clients with comprehensive service guarantees. Whether it's routine maintenance or emergency repairs, our team ensures your assets are handled with the highest level of professionalism and care.

Pricing & Quotations

Transparent and Competitive Rates

Our pricing is primarily quotation-based to ensure you only pay for exactly what your vehicle needs. We provide detailed digital quotes before any work begins, itemizing labor and parts clearly.

  • Minor Service: From $120 (approx.)
  • Major Service: From $350 (approx.)

  • Vehicle make and model complexity
  • Choice between OEM or high-quality aftermarket parts
  • Urgency and after-hours labor requirements
