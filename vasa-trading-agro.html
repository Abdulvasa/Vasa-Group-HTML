Our Services

About the Company

VASA Trading Agro is a cornerstone of the VASA Group, dedicated to the professionalization of the agricultural sector in Mozambique. We specialize in structured operations that ensure the highest standards of reliability and efficiency from farm to market. Our deep local presence allows us to navigate the unique challenges of the Mozambican landscape while maintaining a global perspective on quality and compliance.

With a commitment to transparency and excellence, we provide essential links in the agricultural value chain. Our operations are built on a foundation of trust, leveraging over 8 years of group experience to deliver consistent results for our partners. Whether sourcing raw inputs or managing large-scale storage, VASA Trading Agro is your reliable partner for sustainable agricultural growth.
